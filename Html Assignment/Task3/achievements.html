Achievements

  1. In recognition of its efforts to penetrate globally, HALDIRAM'S bagged the prestigious INTERNATIONAL AWARD FOR FOOD & BEVERAGES awarded by Trade Leaders Club in Barcelona, Italy, Spain in the year 1994.
  2. HALDIRAM'S is honoured to be a member of Snacks Food Association (SFA), Virginia, U.S.A. which comprises of world renowned food product manufacturers.
  3. The Group has also to its credit 'KASHALKAR MEMORIAL AWARD' presented by 'All India Food Preservers Association' (Regd.) in 1996 at its Golden Jubilee Celebration for manufacturing best quality food products.
  4. Haldiram's has also been awarded with APEDA EXPORT AWARD for its highest quality products.
